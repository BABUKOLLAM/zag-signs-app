1. Introduction & System Overview

ZAG SIGNS ERP is a comprehensive cloud-based Enterprise Resource Planning system designed specifically for ZAG SIGNS and all its branches. It manages the complete business lifecycle from capturing sales leads to issuing tax invoices and seamlessly syncing with Tally for accounting.

System Architecture & Accessibility

  • Cloud-Based Platform: Accessible from any browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on desktop, laptop, tablet, or mobile phone
  • No Installation Required: Simply open bprozagcrm.xyz in your browser
  • Progressive Web App: Installable as a native application on mobile devices
  • Real-Time Synchronization: Data synced instantly across all branches
  • Secure Infrastructure: Automatic daily backups with 99.9% uptime SLA
  • Responsive Design: Automatically adapts to any screen size
  • Complete Audit Trail: Every action logged for compliance and dispute resolution

Core Modules Included

  • CRM: Leads & Opportunities — comprehensive prospect management from initial contact to conversion
  • Customers: Master database with complete transaction history and outstanding balance tracking
  • Quotations: Professional quotations with GST support and revision tracking
  • Work Orders: Tickets system connecting front-office with designer workflow
  • Sales & Production: Order management from confirmation through delivery
  • Invoices: Tax invoices with integrated Tally XML export
  • Collections: Payment tracking and receivables management
  • HR: Employee profiles, daily attendance, and leave management
  • Field Operations: Customer visit logs and team activity reports (DAR/WWR/MWR)
  • Inventory: Stock tracking and movement management with low-stock alerts
  • Reports: Daily, weekly, and monthly MIS with analytics and KPIs
  • Admin: User management, system configuration, audit trails

Key Organizational Features

  • Branch-wise data isolation with independent numbering sequences
  • Role-based access control (9 different user roles)
  • Real-time dashboard with live KPIs
  • Global search across all modules (Ctrl+K / Cmd+K)
  • Mobile-first design with full mobile app support

2. Getting Started — Login & Navigation

This section walks you through accessing the system, understanding the dashboard, and navigating through all available modules and features.

Quick Start Guide

Step 1: Open the Application

Open any modern web browser (Google Chrome is recommended for best performance) and navigate to https://bprozagcrm.xyz. The system is fully responsive and works perfectly on desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and mobile phones.

Step 2: Sign In to Your Account

Enter your registered email address and password in the login form. New users will receive their login credentials via email from the IT Admin after their account has been approved. If you haven't received credentials yet, please contact your IT Admin.

Step 3: Dashboard — Your Home Screen

After successful authentication, you'll land on the Dashboard. This page displays important live KPIs and metrics including: Total Revenue (Year-to-Date), Active Orders, Open Leads, Pending Collections, Open Complaints, and Team Tasks.

Step 4: Navigate the System

On Desktop: The left sidebar organizes all modules into logical sections: OVERVIEW (Dashboard, Reports), SALES (Leads, Opportunities, Quotations, Customers), OPERATIONS (Work Orders, Sales Orders, Production), FINANCE (Invoices, Collections), PEOPLE & FIELD (HR, Attendance, Field Visits), REPORTS (MIS, Analytics), and ADMIN (Users, Settings, Audit Trail).

On Mobile: Tap the hamburger menu icon (☰) at the top-left corner to open the sidebar. You can close it again by tapping the icon or swiping from the right.

Step 5: Global Search Functionality

At any time, press Ctrl+K (on Windows) or Cmd+K (on Mac) to open the global search panel. Type any customer name, lead ID, invoice number, quotation number, or keyword to instantly search across all modules simultaneously. This is the fastest way to find information.

Step 6: Install on Mobile as Native App (PWA)

For Android Users: Open Chrome browser → Click the three-dot menu (⋮) at top-right → Select "Add to Home Screen" → Confirm. The app then appears on your home screen and opens full-screen without the browser chrome.

For iPhone Users: Open Safari browser → Click the Share button at bottom → Select "Add to Home Screen" → Give it a name and confirm. The app installs to your home screen and launches full-screen like a native application.

Important Session Information

Session Duration: Your session remains active for 24 hours. If you see the login screen, your session has expired — simply sign in again. The system will remember your password for future logins (if your device is trusted).
Module Access Control: Modules you do not have permission to access are automatically hidden from the sidebar. If you need access to a specific module, contact your IT Admin to request access for your role.

User Preferences & Settings

Click on your user avatar (displayed in the top-right corner) to access: Profile Settings, Preferences, Change Password, Logout, and Help options.

3. End-to-End Business Workflow

LEAD → OPPORTUNITY → CUSTOMER → QUOTATION → WORK ORDER TICKET → SALES ORDER → INVOICE → TALLY

This workflow represents the ideal data flow in ZAG SIGNS ERP. Each step is intelligently connected to the next through one-click action buttons, ensuring that data entered at one stage automatically populates the next stage, eliminating manual re-entry and reducing errors.

Understanding Each Stage Connection

  • On a Lead Row: The [Opp] button creates a new Opportunity | The [Customer] button creates a Customer record | The [Quote] button creates a Quotation
  • On an Opportunity Row: The [Customer] button creates a Customer record | The [Quote] button creates a Quotation with opportunity details
  • On a Customer Row: The [Quote] button creates a Quotation with this customer pre-selected
  • On an Approved/Submitted Quotation: The [Ticket] button creates a Work Order Ticket | The [Invoice] button creates a Tax Invoice with all items automatically copied
  • On an Invoice Row: The [Tally XML] button exports data as Tally XML format | The [Mark Paid] button updates the payment status to PAID

Key Workflow Principles

  • Zero Data Re-entry: Information flows automatically from one stage to the next
  • Data Consistency: All related records remain synchronized automatically
  • Audit Trail: Every action is logged with timestamp and user information
  • Error Reduction: Automatic population eliminates manual entry errors
  • Time Saving: Reduced data entry means faster order processing
Workflow Best Practice: Always follow the workflow in sequence. For example, do not create an Invoice directly — create it from the Quotation using the [Invoice] button. This ensures all data integrity and maintains proper linkage for future reference and audit purposes.

4. Leads & CRM Module

Leads & CRM is the entry point for all new sales. Track every prospect from first contact through qualification and conversion to become a valued customer.

Key Features

  • Status Pipeline: NEW → CONTACTED → QUALIFIED → PROPOSAL → NEGOTIATION → WON/LOST
  • Lead Source tracking (Direct, Referral, Social Media, Trade Show, Website, LinkedIn, Phone, Other)
  • Follow-up Date scheduling with system highlighting today's follow-ups at the top of the list
  • Branch-wise Lead isolation — each branch sees only its own leads
  • Communication History in Notes field for tracking all customer interactions
  • Estimated Value tracking for revenue forecasting
  • Bulk Import/Export capabilities via Excel templates
  • Advanced Filtering and Search across Status, Source, Branch

Step-by-Step Workflow

1. Create Lead

Click "New Lead" button or use bulk import. Required: Name, Phone, Branch. Optional: Company, Email, Source, Estimated Value, Follow-up Date, Assigned Executive

2. Update Status

As you interact with the prospect, update their status from NEW → CONTACTED → QUALIFIED → PROPOSAL → NEGOTIATION → WON or LOST

3. Set Follow-up Dates

Always schedule a follow-up date for the next action. Today's follow-ups are highlighted at the top

4. Convert to Opportunity

When prospect shows interest, click [Opp] button to create an Opportunity record

5. Convert to Customer

When deal is won, click [Customer] to create a permanent Customer record

6. Create Quotation

Click [Quote] to generate a professional quotation directly from the Lead

Best Practice: Always update Lead status and Follow-up Date after every customer interaction. This keeps your sales pipeline accurate and ensures no prospect is forgotten.

7. Quotations Module

Professional quotations with GST support, revision tracking, and one-click conversion to work orders and invoices.

Features

  • Auto-numbered: ZAG/Q/BRANCH/001 (branch-wise, never resets)
  • Professional PDF with Company Logo, Letterhead, GSTIN, Bank Details
  • Line Items: Description, Qty, Unit (Nos/Sqft/Rft/Mtr/Job/Set/Box), Rate per Unit
  • GST Calculation: Enter GST % and system auto-calculates CGST and SGST
  • Discount: Apply at line item or total level
  • Status Workflow: DRAFT → SENT → SUBMITTED → APPROVED → REJECTED
  • Revision History: Create revisions as R1, R2, etc. with change notes
  • Valid Until Date with auto-expiration
  • Quick Actions: [PDF] [Revise] [Ticket] [Invoice]

Step-by-Step Process

1. Create

Click "New Quotation" → Select Customer → Enter Description → Add line items

2. Calculate GST

Enter GST % → System auto-splits as CGST/SGST

3. Preview PDF

Click "PDF" to see professional quotation before sending

4. Update Status

Change status as quotation progresses (SENT, SUBMITTED, APPROVED)

5. Revise if Needed

Use "Revise" for modifications → Creates new quote with -R1, -R2, etc.

6. Convert

Click [Ticket] for Work Order or [Invoice] for Tax Invoice (auto-copies all items)

18. Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I reset my password if I forget it?

Contact your IT Admin with your email ID. They will send you a password reset link via email. Click the link and set a new password.

Q2: Can I access the system from my mobile phone?

Yes, the system works in any modern browser on your phone. For better experience, install it as a PWA: Android users go to Chrome→⋮→"Add to Home Screen"; iPhone users go to Safari→Share→"Add to Home Screen". It then works like a native app.

Q3: How do I export a list from any module to Excel?

Click the "Excel" button on any module list. The currently filtered view downloads as an Excel file with headers and all visible data.

Q4: How do I create a PDF of a quotation or invoice?

In Quotations or Invoices module, click the "PDF" button on any row. A preview opens in print view. Click "Print" and select "Save as PDF" to save it to your computer.

Q5: How do I export an invoice to Tally?

In Invoices module, click the [Tally XML] button on the invoice row. The file downloads automatically as an XML file. In TallyPrime, go to Gateway→Import Data→Vouchers→Enter→Browse to the XML file→Enter. Tally creates the Sales Voucher automatically.

Q6: What happens when my session expires?

Sessions last 24 hours. When expired, you're redirected to the login page. Simply sign in again.

Q7: Can multiple users work in the system at the same time?

Yes, the system supports unlimited concurrent users across all branches with real-time data synchronization.

Q8: How do I add a new user to the system?

Go to Admin→User Management→"Add User". Enter the email, select their role, and send the invitation. They'll receive an email with login instructions.

Q9: How do I import a list of customers from Excel?

Go to Customers module→Click "Import". Download the template, fill in your customer data, then upload the file. The system validates and imports all records.

Q10: How do I track which invoices are still outstanding?

In Customers module, the "Outstanding Balance" column shows dues for each customer. Outstanding invoices appear in the Collections module with PENDING or OVERDUE status.

19. Keyboard Shortcuts & Tips

Essential Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Ctrl+K (Windows) / Cmd+K (Mac) — Opens global search to find customers, leads, invoices, quotations across all modules
  • Escape — Closes any open modal, dialog, dropdown, or search panel
  • Ctrl+P (Windows) / Cmd+P (Mac) — Opens print dialog (useful in Quotations and Invoices modules for saving PDFs)
  • Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) / Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) — Hard refresh — forces reload of latest data from the server
  • Tab — Navigate between form fields
  • Enter — Submit forms or confirm actions

Best Practices for Maximum Productivity

  • Always update Lead status and Follow-up Date: After every customer interaction (call, email, meeting), immediately update the lead status and set a follow-up date for the next action.
  • Create Invoices from Quotations: Never create invoices manually. Always use the [Invoice] button on an approved quotation to ensure all data is automatically copied and properly linked.
  • Raise Work Order Tickets immediately: As soon as a customer confirms an order, create a Work Order Ticket and assign it to the appropriate designer/team. This ensures timely execution.
  • Export to Tally on the same day: When creating an invoice, export it to Tally XML on the same day for clean, day-wise accounting entries.
  • Always use fresh import templates: When doing bulk imports, always download the latest template from the Import dialog. Keep the headers unchanged to avoid validation errors.
  • Record payments immediately: When payment is received, mark the invoice as Paid or Partial immediately in the Collections module to keep outstanding balances current and accurate.
  • Never share login credentials: Each user should have their own login. Sharing credentials makes audit trails unreliable. Contact IT Admin for account issues.
  • Install on mobile for field access: Install the app on your mobile phone (Add to Home Screen) for convenient access during field visits, site surveys, and customer meetings.
  • Use Global Search for quick lookup: Instead of navigating through menus, use Ctrl+K to quickly search for any customer, lead, invoice, or quotation by name or ID.
  • Filter before exporting: Use filters to narrow down the data view before exporting to Excel. This reduces file size and gives you exactly the data you need.
  • Check Dashboard regularly: Review the Dashboard daily for live metrics on revenue, orders, leads, and collections to stay on top of business performance.
  • Set realistic follow-up dates: When creating leads, set follow-up dates based on actual engagement plans. Regular follow-ups are key to converting prospects to customers.
